• About

    MiladyCola is a raffle lottery NFT that upon redeeming could get you 1 MIL :O

    The MiladyCola bottles come in multiple flavors that nod to loveable milady derivatives

    When you open your bottle and redeem it for a cap, you are interacting with a second contract that utilizes chainlink vrf to try and win!

    2/3 of the mint proceeds go towards sweeping MILx on NFTX for our winners, the rest goes to purchasing 2,500LINK and compensating the artist and developer

    The odds of winning will depend on how much MILx we can acquire with the mint proceeds. Once 2/3 of the mint has gone, the odds will be calculated
